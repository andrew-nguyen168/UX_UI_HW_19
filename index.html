My UX Skills

User Research

Begins with being curious about users, conducting qualitative interviews to identify goals, pain-points, and synthesising data to verify that the solution solves the user’s problems.

User Empathy

Developing a deep understanding and empathy for end-users to create designs that not only meet functional requirements but also resonate emotionally with the audience.

Qualitative Interviews

Carefully crafting a detailed interview plan with targeted research questions, and executing engaging 1:1 interviews to identify user goals and needs.

UI Design

Leveraging colour theory, typography, iconography and design principles to create visually stunning interfaces.

Usability Testing

Integrating usability testing into the design process to ensure a continuous feedback loop that refines prototypes and final designs, resulting in products that not only meet but exceeds user expectations.

Wireframing & Prototyping

Creating low-fidelity wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes to visualise and test design concepts, allowing for iterative improvements based on user feedback.
